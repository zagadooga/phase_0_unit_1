Pair Programming

What is pairing?

Solving a problem with another person. Pairing makes tackling programming challenges twice as efficient; two heads are better than one. Video chatting or actually being in person is crucial to pairing.

Why is pair programming important to the site and DBC?

For this site, pair programming solidifes in our mind what pair programming is, so we can explain it to others and apply it later.
Pair progamming accelerates learning, because people can complement each other's shortcomings, so we have a more complete knowledge together. We can also specialize, so people aren't switching languages, and you can divide labor efficiently.

What's a driver and what's a navigator?

A driver gets their hands dirty in the coding, while a navigator googles problems and guides the process as they go.
